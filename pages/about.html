About

The Lore

In Jumpmania, you play as an ordinary adventurer who has wandered into the forests looking for as much gold as you can get your grubby hands on. Fight through hordes of enemies, or challenge yourself to avoid them entirely, as you parkour through a series of platforms and make your way to the final boss!

Game Design

Jumpmania is a platform game where you control the player, an adventurer wandering through a forest.

Traverse the forest by jumping on platforms while fighting (or avoiding) enemies

Buy upgrades from the shop, collect coins and hearts, and make your way to the goal. Watch out for the big boss at the end!

The game only work on the Web(you should be able to play at this point!!)

Rated "E" for everyone

The Idea

This game is part of a final project for the CSS385 Intro to Game Development class of Spring 2024. We(the dev) are all new to game developement and we wanted to make a 2D platformer that simple enough for beginner but also challenging for learning purpose.

We based our project on some of more established game below but with key similarites and differences:

Cuphead

Simularities: Platforming and shooting things

Differences: No bosses, you are not a cup.

Mario

Simularities: Platforming towards a finish line with items and enemies

Differences: You are (probably) not a plumber.

Kirby

Similarities: Platformer with finish line and enemies and items.

Differences: Can't fly (maybe) and not a rotund widdle pink alien.
